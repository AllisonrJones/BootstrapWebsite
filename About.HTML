    Ceramics

    Upstream Creations is the small business I created when starting to sell my work as a professional potter. The name springs for the idea that the things you want in life exist somewhere against the current. Achieving your goals can sometimes seem like an upstream swim, but always worth the efforts. See Upstream Creations

    Pottery Lane is both the place where I took my first ceramics class in 2019, and where I began to teach and help grow community in 2020. My time in the studio is spent teaching students and teachers, learning from students and teachers, as well as creating SOP procedures, loading kilns and mixing glazes Pottery Lane

    Flux GR was an artist market and small business owner opportunity that I helped produce this year. We taught new artists how to market themselve, how to prepare for success, and give them the cofidence boost that is sometimes necessary to show their work for the first time.
